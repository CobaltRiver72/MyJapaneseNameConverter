About Us

Dedicated to accurate and culturally respectful Japanese name translation

Our Mission

My Japanese Name Translator is dedicated to providing accurate English-to-Japanese name conversion using kanji, romaji, and deep cultural context. We believe that a name is more than just a word — it carries identity, heritage, and meaning across languages and cultures.

Our mission is to bridge the linguistic gap between English and Japanese naming conventions, ensuring every translation is both phonetically precise and culturally meaningful.

We combine linguistic expertise with cultural sensitivity to deliver Japanese name translations that honor both the original name and Japanese naming traditions.

Our Expertise

Our Japanese name translations are grounded in deep understanding of:

  • Japanese Phonology — Mapping English sounds to Japanese mora structure with precision
  • Kanji Semantics — Choosing characters that convey appropriate meaning and cultural resonance
  • Naming Conventions — Following traditional Japanese rules for name structure, gender associations, and formality
  • Hepburn Romanization — Using the internationally recognized standard for Japanese-to-English transliteration
  • Cultural Sensitivity — Ensuring all translations respect Japanese traditions and social norms

Why Accuracy Matters

Japanese names carry deep cultural and linguistic significance. Each kanji character contributes phonetic sound, semantic meaning, and cultural resonance. An inaccurate or inappropriate name translation can cause misunderstanding or unintended offense.

That's why we take a meticulous approach to every conversion — analyzing phonetics, selecting culturally appropriate kanji, and providing clear explanations so you understand exactly what your Japanese name means.
